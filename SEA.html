My Work at SEA

During my tenure at Systems Engineering & Assessment from May 2020 to December 2022, I played a pivotal role in delivering a wide array of defense and training software projects. Working both collaboratively within teams and independently, I took on complex tasks that required a deep understanding of various technologies and development practices. My responsibilities included delivering products directly to customer sites, ensuring they were accompanied by comprehensive delivery documentation for seamless implementation and support.

One of the key challenges I embraced was managing multiple projects simultaneously. This often required adept context switching between different programming languages and platforms, demonstrating my versatility and ability to maintain high performance under pressure. My projects spanned both virtual reality and standard screen-based software, where I consistently developed innovative and cutting-edge solutions tailored to the unique needs of defense and training applications.

I also contributed to the development of an efficient in-house Integrated Development Environment (IDE) for bespoke solutions, which streamlined our workflow and enhanced productivity. As the primary C# and front-end developer for augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) projects, I leveraged my expertise to push the boundaries of immersive technology and deliver high-quality, user-centric software solutions.

Throughout my time at Systems Engineering & Assessment, I was committed to advancing the capabilities of our defense and training software. My efforts not only improved the technical aspects of our projects but also ensured that our solutions were practical, reliable, and effective in real-world scenarios. This experience honed my skills in software development, project management, and client relations, preparing me for future challenges in the tech industry.
