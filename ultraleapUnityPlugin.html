My Work at Ultraleap

During my time as a Unity Software Engineer at Ultraleap from December 2022 to August 2024, I played a key role in the development and maintenance of the Ultraleap Unity Plugin for hand tracking. I was responsible for creating innovative features and demos tailored for customers and trade shows, significantly contributing to the advancement of pose detection and physical hand functionalities within the plugin. My work involved communicating with low-level hardware APIs to implement new features and push the boundaries of hand tracking technology in Unity.

I ensured the creation and upkeep of comprehensive developer documentation, including a major refactor, and developed scalable tools using SOLID principles and Agile processes. I built applications for all Android VR platforms and Windows standalone, and closely integrated my work with MRTK, Unity XRHands, and Unity XRI. My efforts at Ultraleap advanced the capabilities of hand tracking technology, providing valuable solutions and enhancements to the field of virtual reality.
