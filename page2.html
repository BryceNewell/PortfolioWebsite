Country Road

A semi-infinite co-op runner

2nd place at the Global Game Jam 2019. A co-operative runner game in which up to 4 players can control a pack of horses pulling a cart in order to get back home.

This game was created with the idea of "home" in mind.  You play as a horse, pulling a cart back to your homestead in the wild wild west.  The game was created by a group of 5 people during the 48 hours of the Global Game Jam 2019, placing second at the UK's biggest Jam site.

With a procedurally generating map, ever-increasing speed and perrilous terrain, you must try to take the correct route home, opting for either left or right, based on the signs you see throughout the game.
