About me

Hi, I'm Bryce Newell, a dedicated software engineer with a First Class BSc Honours degree in Games Technology from the University of the West of England. I specialize in game development and interactive technologies, with a strong background in Unity and C++.

Currently, I work at Ultraleap, where I develop and maintain the Unity Plugin for hand tracking, creating innovative features and demos. My previous experience includes developing defense and training software at Systems Engineering & Assessment, and co-founding BruwSoft, a medical research software company.

I'm proficient in languages like C#, C++, and SQL, and have extensive experience with Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender, and Visual Studio. I enjoy working on game projects, like my current pirate ship building survival game in Unreal Engine 5, and my hobbies include longboarding, Japanese cars, Dungeons and Dragons, and rock climbing.
