Country Roads (Game Jam)

Team Work

Working with 4 other team members over the course of a game jam weekend to create something we were all proud of.

Dedication

Late nights and early mornings to bring our vision to life.

Fast Iteration

As the game jam was over just 2 days, we had to implement and iterate on our ideas in a very short timeframe.

The Game

This game was created with the theme of "home" in mind. In it, you play as a horse pulling a cart back to your homestead in the Wild West. Developed by a team of five during the 48-hour Global Game Jam 2019, it earned second place at the UK's largest jam site.

The game features a procedurally generated map with ever-increasing speed and perilous terrain. Players must choose the correct route home by deciding to go left or right based on the signs encountered throughout the journey. This project showcased our team's ability to quickly develop engaging and challenging gameplay under tight deadlines.
